    • My individual illustrations depict the beauty of Filipino women in modern, foreign settings - filled with nature and growth. As a young artist, I delve deeper into understanding the struggles of identity as a child born in Canada with South-East Asian, immigrant parents, as well as the transition into adulthood in a world that is constantly changing.

    I experiment with a variety of mediums, including watercolour and digital painting (Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and After Effects)
